About us

A small farm situated between Edinburgh and Carlisle has been transformed into a visitor attraction which includes a shop selling local produce and plants, small zoo, cafe, play park and rides. Dalscone started out as a Farm Shop 25 years ago before we expanded into an attraction! Dalscone Farm Park is the proud recipient of the Visit Scotland 5 Star Visitor Attraction Award and is Dumfries' premier day out for all the family! Our Farm Park offers you the chance to meet our animals and even give them some tasty treats! There are many attractions and fun for all ages!
