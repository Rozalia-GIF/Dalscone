Plants and Produce at Dalscone Farm Park

Our shelves are stocked with a wide range of fresh fruit and vegetables, butchery, farm-fresh milk, free-range eggs, dairy produce, confectionary, preserves and pantry staples. A full list of our producers will be available here soon. We have sourced our product ranges from all over Scotland to provide our customers with the best selection, considering seasonality and quality.

Produce

We have been growing for over 20 years and every day we harvest fresh seasonal fruit and vegetables to stock the shop – vegetables are very often still dripping! Wandering through the shop, the smell of freshly cooked bread will hit you which is a true reminder of the quality and freshness of our bakery products. See the bakers and pastry chefs busily producing an array of products right in front of your eyes, although never too busy to answer any questions you may have!

Plants

Our plant area has a very traditional feel with an extensive range of A to Z shrubs, perennials, trees, roses, alpines and lots more. We also stock a good selection of acid loving, Japanese style and large specimen type plants. Everything you need for an experienced or a beginner gardener. Our plant area staff are either keen gardeners themselves or have an interest in horticulture. They are always on hand for help and expert advice or just for some garden gossip and inspiration.

Tools

It is important to have the right high-quality tools and accessories to make your job easier. We have tools and equipment for every job, no matter how big or small.  We also stock a variety of fertilisers and weedkillers and will offer advice on natural and organic solutions should you prefer not to use chemicals in your garden.
